In two of my previous articles I described my experience developing my first Opera Extension LinkMoi, and I am now proud to say, LinkMoi has reached its first landmark, 100+downloads.

I do admit there were a few bugs in the initial result, but it was all Javascript's fault, I swear I had nothing to do with it. ;)

The first released version, broke the javascript on a few pages such as http://play.google.com (You know you need my extension if you are too lazy to copy paste this link ;) ).

So after trying to fix the bug after 6 hours of hit and trial debugging, I finally released a stable version which has been accepted by Opera and is now listed on http://addons.opera.com.

You can download my extension here:

https://addons.opera.com/en-gb/extensions/details/linkmoi/?display=en
